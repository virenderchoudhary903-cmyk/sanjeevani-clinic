About Sanjeevani

Aapka Apna Vishwasniya Hospital

Sanjeevani Multispeciality Hospital pichle 10 saalon se aam janta ko behtareen aur sasta ilaaj uplabdh kara raha hai. Hamara manna hai ki acche ilaaj ke liye kisi ko bade shehar jane ki zaroorat nahi padni chahiye.

Hamare paas expert doctors ki team, aadhunik machines, aur 24 ghante emergency suvidha uplabdh hai. Hamara staff har patient ki poori dekhbhal karta hai.

Our Departments

Har Bimari Ka Sateek Ilaaj

🩺 General Medicine

Mausami bukhar, Dengue, Malaria, BP, aur Sugar jaisi sabhi aam bimariyon ka behtareen ilaaj.

🦴 Orthopedics (Haddi Rog)

Haddiyon ka tootna, jodon ka dard (Arthritis), aur spine ki samasyaon ka aadhunik ilaaj.

👶 Pediatrics (Bal Rog)

Navjat shishu aur bacchon ki bimariyon ke liye special care, vaccination, aur NICU suvidha.

👁️ Ophthalmology (Netra Rog)

Motiyabind ka operation, aankhon ki janch, aur chashme ka number check karne ki suvidha.

🤰 Gynecology (Stree Rog)

Mahilao ki samasyayein, pregnancy care, aur normal/cesarean delivery ki suvidha.

🦷 Dentistry (Dant Rog)

Danton me dard, root canal, aur daant nikalne/lagane ka dard-rahit ilaaj.
